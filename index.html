ABOUT US

About Us

B-able Storytelling is a storytelling club that records stories, both long and short, and sends them mainly to facilities for visually-impaired people, orphanages, and child hospitals. Our club members each choose our favorite fairy tales and fables that we had enjoyed reading during our childhood. We practice reading them clearly and expressively to be able to deliver both story and emotion to the listener. After a few weeks of practice, by the end of the quarter, we record the practiced tales in our best voices. These recordings would be sent to various places, such as orphanages and schools and care places for the visually impaired.

Mission

Our mission is to give many children the joy of exploring different stories. In Korea, there are many children who cannot read English books and because they are visually-impaired. Even though audiobooks exist, not all audiobooks are provided for, especially in English. Therefore, we decided to record ourselves reading certain books and donate these recordings to different places, such as orphanages, care places for visually-impaired people, and if the situation permits, child hospitals.

Contact

Our club would be more than welcome to receive story recordings from different people. These stories can be any type of story: long books, short stories, and fairytales. Please contact us if you have any questions, or would like more information!
